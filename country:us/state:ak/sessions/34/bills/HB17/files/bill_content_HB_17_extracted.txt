AS 39.35.370(a) is amended to read: 04 (a) Subject to AS 39.35.450, a terminated employee is eligible for a normal 05 retirement benefit 06 (1) [AT AGE 60] with at least five years of credited service 07 (A) at age 60; or 08 (B) at any age if the terminated employee is a veteran rated 09 by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs as permanently and 10 totally disabled for a service-connected condition; 11 (2) with at least 20 years of credited service as a peace officer or 12 firefighter; or 13 (3) with at least 30 years of credited service for all other employees. 14 * Sec. 2. AS 39.35.340(d), 39.35.340(i), and 39.35.541(b) are repealed.
