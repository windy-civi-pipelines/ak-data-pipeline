CSHB 191(RES): "An Act establishing the Alaska Invasive Species Council in the Department of Fish and Game; relating to management of invasive species; and providing for an effective date." 

00                      CS FOR HOUSE BILL NO. 191(RES)                                                                    
01 "An Act establishing the Alaska Invasive Species Council in the Department of Fish and                                  
02 Game; relating to management of invasive species; and providing for an effective date."                                 
03 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
04    * Section 1. AS 16.20 is amended by adding new sections to read:                                                 
05                  Article 6. Management of Invasive Species.                                                           
06 Sec. 16.20.800. Alaska Invasive Species Council. (a) The Alaska Invasive                                              
07 Species Council is an advisory council established in the department to facilitate                                      
08 efforts by the department, the Department of Natural Resources, the Department of                                       
09 Environmental Conservation, the Department of Transportation and Public Facilities,                                     
10       and other natural-resource-related governmental entities operating in the state to                                
11       manage invasive species in the state by                                                                           
12                 (1)  facilitating cooperation between state, federal, tribal, and local                                 
13       agencies and nongovernmental organizations;                                                                       
14                 (2)  assisting in coordination of activities to manage invasive species,                                



01       including those undertaken by local governments and nongovernmental organizations;                                
02                 (3)  recommending coordinated interagency strategies and policies                                       
03       related to management of invasive species, including                                                              
04                      (A)  importation, possession, and use of invasive species;                                         
05                      (B)  classification and listing of non-native species; and                                         
06                      (C)  management of pathways associated with transfer of                                            
07            invasive species;                                                                                            
08                 (4)  upon request of a state agency, providing guidance on prioritizing                                 
09       the state agency's responses to occurrences of invasive species and how funds made                                
10       available under AS 16.20.820 may be best used;                                                                    
11 (5)  recommending expenditures from the invasive species response                                                       
12       fund;                                                                                                             
13 (6)  providing technical information and assistance to persons engaged                                                  
14       in regional and national invasive species management efforts.                                                     
15            (b)  Except as provided in (c) of this section, the council consists of                                      
16 (1)  the following members appointed by the governor to three-year                                                      
17       terms:                                                                                                            
18 (A)  a representative of the University of Alaska who is                                                                
19            involved in research relating to, or management of, invasive species;                                        
20 (B)  a representative of an Alaska tribal entity who is involved                                                        
21            in management of invasive species;                                                                           
22 (C)  a representative of a statewide organization focused on                                                            
23            prevention, detection, and management of invasive species;                                                   
24 (D)  a representative of an organization involved with                                                                  
25            prevention, detection, and management of marine invasive species in the state;                               
26 (E)  a representative involved in invasive species management                                                           
27 through a soil and water conservation district established under AS 41.10.130,                                          
28 a conservation district established by an Alaska Native organization, or an                                             
29            organization concerned with the health of watersheds in the state;                                           
30 (2)  one member of the house of representatives appointed by the                                                        
31       speaker of the house of representatives;                                                                          



01                 (3)  one member of the senate appointed by the president of the senate;                                 
02                 (4)  the commissioner of each of the following departments or the                                       
03       commissioner's designee:                                                                                          
04                      (A)  the Department of Fish and Game;                                                              
05                      (B)  the Department of Natural Resources;                                                          
06                      (C)  the Department of Environmental Conservation;                                                 
07                      (D)  the Department of Transportation and Public Facilities.                                       
08            (c)  Members of the council serving under (b)(2) - (4) of this section serve as                              
09       nonvoting members. The council may, as the council finds necessary, invite additional                             
10 persons to be nonvoting advisory members of the council. The council may invite                                         
11 representatives or members of federal entities, local government organizations, tribal                                  
12 governments, institutions of higher learning in the state, and other organizations with                                 
13 expertise relating to invasive species to join the council under this subsection. The                                   
14 council may prescribe procedures for the participation of persons added to the council                                  
15       under this subsection.                                                                                            
16 (d)  The council shall adopt bylaws to govern its operation. The bylaws must                                            
17       provide procedures for                                                                                            
18                 (1)  decision making; and                                                                               
19                 (2)  establishing subcommittees and advisory committees.                                                
20 (e)  The council shall elect a chair and vice chair from among the members                                              
21 appointed under (b)(1) of this section. The council shall meet at least quarterly at the                                
22 call of the chair. A majority of the voting members of the council constitute a quorum.                                 
23       Meetings and council votes may be held by audio or video teleconference.                                          
24 (f)  Members of the council serve without compensation and are not entitled to                                          
25 per diem and travel expenses. However, a member appointed under (b)(4) of this                                          
26       section is entitled to per diem and travel expenses as a state employee.                                          
27 (g)  By January 15 of each odd-numbered year, the council shall prepare and                                             
28 present to the legislature an updated five-year strategic plan that addresses the effects                               
29 of invasive species on the state's natural resources and economy, states the council's                                  
30 suggested priorities for addressing invasive species, and recommends legislation and                                    
31 funding required to implement the council's priorities. The council shall transmit the                                  



01 strategic plan to the senate secretary and the chief clerk of the house of representatives                              
02       and notify the legislature that the plan is available.                                                            
03 (h)  By January 15 of each year, the council shall prepare and present an                                               
04 annual report on invasive species active in the state, the effects of the activity, and any                             
05 measures taken to manage invasive species. The report must include other issues and                                     
06 concerns related to invasive species that the council determines are of value to                                        
07 policymakers. The council shall submit the report to the governor, the department, the                                  
08 Department of Natural Resources, the Department of Environmental Conservation, the                                      
09 Department of Transportation and Public Facilities, and the senate secretary and chief                                  
10 clerk of the house of representatives, and notify the legislature that the report is                                    
11       available.                                                                                                        
12 (i)  Council members appointed under (b)(1) of this section serve staggered                                             
13 three-year terms and until a successor is appointed. A vacancy on the council shall be                                  
14 filled in the same manner as the original appointment. If a vacancy occurs, the                                         
15 governor shall appoint a member for the unexpired portion of the term within 60 days                                    
16       after the occurrence of the vacancy.                                                                              
17 Sec. 16.20.810. Invasive species response fund. The invasive species                                                  
18 response fund is established as an account in the general fund. The fund consists of                                    
19 appropriations made to the fund. Money appropriated to the fund does not lapse. The                                     
20 department may use money appropriated to the fund to manage invasive species,                                           
21       without further appropriation.                                                                                    
22            Sec. 16.20.850. Definitions. In AS 16.20.800 - 16.20.850,                                                  
23                 (1)  "council" means the Alaska Invasive Species Council;                                               
24                 (2)  "department" means the Department of Fish and Game;                                                
25 (3)  "invasive species" means a non-native species the introduction of                                                  
26 which causes or is likely to cause economic harm, environmental harm, or harm to                                        
27       human health;                                                                                                     
28 (4)  "manage invasive species" means to take actions to prevent or                                                      
29 minimize harmful effects of invasive species on the economy, the environment, public                                    
30       health, infrastructure, and cultural heritage;                                                                    
31 (5)  "non-native species" includes any biological material capable of                                                   



01       propagating a species that is not native to the state or an ecosystem in the state.                               
02    * Sec. 2. AS 16.20.800, 16.20.810, and 16.20.850 are repealed July 1, 2035.                                        
03    * Sec. 3. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to                          
04 read:                                                                                                                   
05 APPOINTMENT OF MEMBERS AND CHAIR AND TIMING AND NUMBER OF                                                               
06 MEETINGS REQUIRED IN INITIAL YEAR. (a) Notwithstanding AS 16.20.810(i), added                                           
07 by sec. 1 of this Act, the governor shall appoint the initial members of the Alaska Invasive                            
08 Species Council required under AS 16.20.800(b)(1) within 60 days after the effective date of                            
09 this Act. The governor shall appoint the members to staggered terms as follows:                                         
10            (1)  two members shall be appointed to three-year terms;                                                     
11            (2)  two members shall be appointed to two-year terms; and                                                   
12            (3)  one member shall be appointed to a one-year term.                                                       
13       (b)  Notwithstanding AS 16.20.800(e), added by sec. 1 of this Act, the Alaska Invasive                            
14 Species Council shall                                                                                                   
15            (1)  hold its first meeting within 90 days after the appointment of members                                  
16 under (a) of this section; and                                                                                          
17            (2)  meet at least four times in the 12 months following the appointment of                                  
18 members under (a) of this section.                                                                                      
19    * Sec. 4. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
