AS 11.81.370 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 05 (d) A peace officer may not knowingly use a carotid restraint, tracheal choke, 06 or other restraint that impedes another person's breathing or circulation of blood in a 07 manner likely to produce a loss of consciousness except in situations for which deadly 08 force is authorized by law.
