AS 14.12.050 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 05 (d) The governing body of a municipality may by ordinance alter the terms of 06 office provided for in this section. 07 * Sec. 2. AS 29.20.300(a) is amended to read: 08 (a) Each municipal school district has a school board. Except as provided in 09 (b) or (c) of this section, members of a school board are elected at the regular election 10 for three-year terms and until their successors take office. Members are elected at 11 large unless a different method of election has been approved by the voters in a regular 12 election. 13 * Sec. 3. AS 29.20.300 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 14 (c) The governing body of a municipality may by ordinance approved by the 01 voters alter the terms of office provided for in (a) of this section. 02 * Sec. 4. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
