ALASKA INDUSTRIAL DEVELOPMENT AND EXPORT AUTHORITY. 05 The sum of $50,000,000 is appropriated from the general fund to the Alaska Industrial 06 Development and Export Authority for the reimbursement of the Alaska Liquified Natural 07 Gas pipeline project front-end engineering and design expenditures for the fiscal years ending 08 June 30, 2025, June 30, 2026, and June 30, 2027. 09 * Sec. 2. RETROACTIVITY. If this Act takes effect after June 30, 2025, this Act is 02 retroactive to July 1, 2024. 03 * Sec. 5. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
