AS 44.19 is amended by adding a new section to read: 05 Sec. 44.19.032. Governor's mansion. The Office of the Governor shall rent 06 the governor's mansion on a short-term basis when the legislature is not in session and 07 the governor has not reserved the mansion in advance. If the legislature is called into 08 special session, an agreement to rent the mansion during the special session is 09 unenforceable. The Office of the Governor shall adopt regulations establishing a 10 system for making the governor's mansion available for a person to rent and the fee 11 charged for renting the mansion. 12 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
