04 BE IT RESOLVED by the House of Representatives that a House Special Committee 05 on Energy is established to study issues relating to energy use and energy development; and 06 be it 07 FURTHER RESOLVED that the Speaker of the House of Representatives shall 08 determine the number of representatives to be members of the committee and shall appoint 09 the members and designate a member or members to chair the committee; and be it 10 FURTHER RESOLVED that the House Special Committee on Energy may meet 11 during and between sessions of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature and is terminated 12 on the convening of the First Regular Session of the Thirty-Fifth Alaska State Legislature.
