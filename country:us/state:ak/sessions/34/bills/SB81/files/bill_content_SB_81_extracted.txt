AS 14.25.070(d) is amended to read: 06 (d) Notwithstanding (a) of this section, the annual employer contribution rate 07 may not be less than the rate sufficient to allow payment of 08 (1) the employer normal cost; 09 (2) an amount calculated at the rate certified under 10 AS 37.10.220(a)(8) for liquidating past service liability of the plan; and 11 (3) the employer contributions required under AS 14.25.350 and 12 AS 39.30.370. 13 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
