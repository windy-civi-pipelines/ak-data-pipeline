01 Relating to the sale of alcohol; relating to the posting of warning signs for alcoholic                                 
02 beverages; and providing for an effective date.

05            (c)  The board may issue or renew a license under this section only if the                                   
06                 (1)  board determines that                                                                              
07                      (A)  the licensed premises is a bona fide restaurant as defined in
