CSHB 133(CRA): "An Act establishing a 30-day deadline for the payment of contracts under the State Procurement Code; relating to the calculation of interest owed to a prime contractor; establishing deadlines for the payment of grants, contracts, and reimbursement agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and Alaska Native organizations; and providing for an effective date." 

00                      CS FOR HOUSE BILL NO. 133(CRA)                                                                    
01 "An Act establishing a 30-day deadline for the payment of contracts under the State                                     
02 Procurement Code; relating to the calculation of interest owed to a prime contractor;                                   
03 establishing deadlines for the payment of grants, contracts, and reimbursement                                          
04 agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and Alaska Native organizations;                                 
05 and providing for an effective date."                                                                                   
06 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
07    * Section 1. AS 36.30 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                
08            Sec. 36.30.970. Payment deadline and interest. (a) An agency shall pay a                                   
09       contractor for satisfactory performance on a contract within 30 calendar days after the                           
10 agency receives a payment request from the contractor that complies with the contract.                                  
11 (b)  If a contractor is not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency                                         
12 shall pay interest on the unpaid amount of the required payment from the 31st calendar                                  
13 day after the date the agency receives a payment request under (a) of this section at an                                



01       interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a).                                               
02 (c)  If an agency withholds payment because of unsatisfactory performance or                                            
03 if the payment request made under (a) of this section does not comply with the                                          
04 requirements of the contract, within eight working days after receipt of the payment                                    
05 request the agency shall notify the contractor in writing stating specifically why                                      
06 payment is being withheld and what remedial actions may be taken by the contractor                                      
07       to receive the full payment.                                                                                      
08 (d)  If the notification by the agency required by (c) of this section does not                                         
09 comply with (c) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount                                   
10 from the eighth working day after receipt of the initial payment request until the                                      
11       agency provides notice that does comply with (c) of this section.                                                 
12 (e)  If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the                                         
13 withheld amount within 30 calendar days after the contractor satisfactorily completes                                   
14 the remedial actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not paid within                                
15 the 30 calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount from the                                     
16 30th calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a).                             
17 (f)  The obligation to pay interest under this section does not apply to                                                
18       retainage.                                                                                                        
19 (g)  This section does not apply to a public construction or public works                                               
20       contract subject to AS 36.90.200 or an agreement subject to AS 36.90.310.                                         
21 (h)  In this section, "contractor" means a person who has been awarded a                                                
22       contract with an agency.                                                                                          
23    * Sec. 2. AS 36.90.200(b) is amended to read:                                                                      
24 (b)  If the prime contractor is not paid as required by (a) of this section, the                                        
25 state or political subdivision shall pay interest on the unpaid amount of the required                                  
26 payment from the [21ST CALENDAR] day [AFTER THE DATE REQUIRED FOR]                                                      
27 payment is due under (a) of this section at an interest rate that is equal to the amount                            
28       set out in AS 45.45.010(a).                                                                                       
29    * Sec. 3. AS 36.90 is amended by adding a new section to article 4 to read:                                        
30 Sec. 36.90.310. Payment deadline and interest for grants and                                                          
31 reimbursement agreements to nonprofit organizations, municipalities, and                                              



01 Alaska Native organizations. (a) An agency shall pay a nonprofit organization,                                        
02 municipality, or Alaska Native organization for satisfactory performance of a grant or                                  
03 reimbursement agreement within 30 calendar days after the agency receives a payment                                     
04 request from the nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization                                    
05 that complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement. If the agency uses federal                                     
06 funds to pay the grant or reimbursement agreement, the agency shall pay for                                             
07 satisfactory performance within 30 calendar days after the agency receives a payment                                    
08 request that complies with the grant or reimbursement agreement, or within 30                                           
09       calendar days after the agency receives the federal funds, whichever is later.                                    
10 (b)  If a nonprofit organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization is                                        
11 not paid as required by (a) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the unpaid                                
12 amount of the required payment at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in                                 
13       AS 45.45.010(a) from                                                                                              
14 (1)  the 30th calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment                                                 
15       request under (a) of this section if the agency uses federal funds to make the payment;                           
16 (2)  the 30th calendar day after the date the agency receives a payment                                                 
17 request under (a) of this section for all other payments for grants and reimbursement                                   
18       agreements.                                                                                                       
19 (c)  If an agency withholds payment because of unsatisfactory performance or                                            
20 if the payment request made under (a) of this section does not comply with the                                          
21 requirements of the grant or reimbursement agreement, within eight working days                                         
22 after receipt of the payment request the agency shall notify the nonprofit organization,                                
23 municipality, or Alaska Native organization in writing stating specifically why the                                     
24 payment is being withheld and what remedial actions may be taken by the nonprofit                                       
25       organization, municipality, or Alaska Native organization to receive the full payment.                            
26 (d)  If the notification by the agency required by (c) of this section does not                                         
27 comply with (c) of this section, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount                                   
28 from the eighth working day after receipt of the initial payment request until the                                      
29       agency provides notice that does comply with (c) of this section.                                                 
30 (e)  If payment is withheld under (c) of this section, the agency shall pay the                                         
31 withheld amount within 30 calendar days after the nonprofit organization,                                               



01 municipality, or Alaska Native organization satisfactorily completes the remedial                                       
02 actions identified in the notice. If the withheld amount is not paid within the 30                                      
03 calendar days, the agency shall pay interest on the withheld amount from the 30th                                       
04       calendar day at an interest rate that is equal to the rate set out in AS 45.45.010(a).                            
05            (f)  In this section,                                                                                        
06                 (1)  "agency" has the meaning given in AS 36.30.990;                                                    
07                 (2)  "Alaska Native organization" has the meaning given in                                              
08       AS 18.66.020(d);                                                                                                  
09                 (3)  "grant" means a legal instrument of financial assistance between an                                
10 agency and a nonstate entity that is used to enter into a relationship for which the                                    
11       principal purpose is to provide funds to the nonstate entity; "grant" does not include                            
12 (A)  a cooperative agreement that provides for substantial                                                              
13 involvement between the agency and the recipient in carrying out the activity                                           
14            contemplated by the award;                                                                                   
15 (B)  a legal instrument used by an agency to acquire property or                                                        
16            services for the direct benefit of an agency;                                                                
17                      (C)  cash assistance to an individual;                                                             
18                      (D)  a subsidy;                                                                                    
19                      (E)  a loan;                                                                                       
20                      (F)  a loan guarantee;                                                                             
21                      (G)  insurance;                                                                                    
22 (H)  state funding that is required annually and is calculated                                                          
23            through a formula set in statute;                                                                            
24                      (I)  a permanent fund dividend; or                                                                 
25 (J)  a grant appropriated to a municipality under AS 37.05.315                                                          
26            or a named recipient under AS 37.05.316;                                                                     
27 (4)  "nonprofit organization" has the meaning given in                                                                  
28       AS 38.05.097(c).                                                                                                  
29    * Sec. 4. This Act takes effect July 1, 2026.
