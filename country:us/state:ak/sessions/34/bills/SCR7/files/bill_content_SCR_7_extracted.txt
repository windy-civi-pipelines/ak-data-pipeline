relating to allowable absences for eligibility for a permanent fund dividend; and relating to electronic notice of debt collection executed on a permanent fund dividend. 00 SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 7 01 Suspending Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State 02 Legislature, concerning House Bill No. 75, relating to national criminal history record 03 checks for certain employees of the Department of Revenue; relating to allowable 04 absences for eligibility for a permanent fund dividend; and relating to electronic notice 05 of debt collection executed on a permanent fund dividend. 06 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 07 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 08 Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, regarding 09 changes to the title of a bill, are suspended in consideration of House Bill No. 75, relating to 10 national criminal history record checks for certain employees of the Department of Revenue; 11 relating to allowable absences for eligibility for a permanent fund dividend; and relating to 12 electronic notice of debt collection executed on a permanent fund dividend.
