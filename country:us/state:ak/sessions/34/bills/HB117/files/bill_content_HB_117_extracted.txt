AS 16.05.251 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 04 (j) Two or more set gillnet entry permit holders in a salmon fishery may fish 05 their respective authorized set gillnet gear as a cooperative. However, a permit holder 06 may not operate a unit of gear belonging to another permit holder without the other 07 permit holder being present at all times and actively engaged in the operation. Not 08 more than 10 set gillnet entry permit holders may fish as one cooperative. Fish 09 harvested under this subsection do not need to be stored or delivered separately on the 10 basis of the individual entry permits fished cooperatively. The Board of Fisheries shall 11 adopt regulations to implement this subsection. The regulations must 12 (1) require set gillnet permit holders fishing multiple units of gear as 13 part of a cooperative to register with the department; 14 (2) specify how a set gillnet permit holder must report fish harvested 15 when fishing cooperatively with other permit holders. 01 * Sec. 2. AS 16.05.680 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 02 (c) For the purposes of this section, a commercial set gillnet entry permit 03 holder who is a member of a cooperative authorized under AS 16.05.251(j) may sell 04 all salmon that was harvested in combination with other permit holder members of the 05 cooperative. Fish harvested collectively by the cooperative may be sold by a single 06 permit holder member of the cooperative or by a transporter acting as the cooperative's 07 agent. 08 * Sec. 3. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
