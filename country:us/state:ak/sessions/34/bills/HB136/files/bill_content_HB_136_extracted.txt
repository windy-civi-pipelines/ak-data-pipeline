AS 42.40.350(b) is amended to read: 04 (b) Railroad utility corridors shall be of a width at least 100 feet on both sides 05 of the centerline of the extended main or branch line, unless the corporation does not 06 own or control sufficient land to allow a corridor of that width. Railroad utility 07 corridors may be surveyed by the metes and bounds method. The corporation may not 08 convey its entire interest in land within a utility corridor except as provided in 09 AS 42.40.285, 42.40.370(d) and 42.40.400. However, except as provided in 10 AS 42.40.415, the corporation may lease, subject to AS 42.40.285 and (d) of this 11 section, grant easements in or permits for, or otherwise authorize use of portions of a 12 utility corridor for transportation, communication, and transmission purposes and 13 support functions associated with those purposes, and for commercial and other uses 14 authorized under this chapter if the use does not restrict other parallel uses of the 15 utility corridor. 01 * Sec. 2. AS 42.40.350(c) is amended to read: 02 (c) Except as provided in AS 42.40.415, the [THE] corporation may lease, 03 subject to AS 42.40.285 and (d) of this section, grant easements in or permits for, or 04 otherwise authorize use of portions of rail land. However, the corporation may not 05 convey its entire interest in rail land except as provided in AS 42.40.285, 42.40.352, 06 42.40.370(d), and 42.40.400. 07 * Sec. 3. AS 42.40 is amended by adding a new section to read: 08 Sec. 42.40.415. Use of easement. The corporation shall allow an owner of real 09 property subject to an easement in favor of the corporation to use the property in a 10 manner that does not unreasonably interfere with the corporation's use of the property 11 for the purpose of the easement.
