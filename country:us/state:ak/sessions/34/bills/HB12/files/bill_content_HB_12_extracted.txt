AS 14.17 is amended by adding a new section to article 1 to read: 04 Sec. 14.17.495. Free breakfast and lunch in public schools. Subject to 05 appropriation, and in addition to the state aid received under this chapter, the 06 department shall provide to a district sufficient funding to allow the district to provide 07 breakfast and lunch to any student in the district who requests the meal, at no cost to 08 the student. The department shall maximize the state's receipt of federal financial 09 assistance that may be available for providing breakfast and lunch to students.
