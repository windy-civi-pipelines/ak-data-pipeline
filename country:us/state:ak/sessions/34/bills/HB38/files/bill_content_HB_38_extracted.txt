AS 39.30.170(a) is amended to read: 06 (a) An employer in the teachers' retirement system (AS 14.25) that does 07 not participate in the federal social security system may become a participating 08 employer in the employee benefits program under AS 39.30.150 - 39.30.180 [, IF 09 (1) THE EMPLOYER PARTICIPATES AS AN EMPLOYER IN 10 THE PUBLIC EMPLOYEES' RETIREMENT SYSTEM UNDER AS 39.35; AND 11 (2) THE EMPLOYER 12 (A) IS ELIGIBLE FOR MEMBERSHIP IN BUT HAS 13 NEVER ELECTED TO BECOME A MEMBER OF THE FEDERAL 14 SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM; OR 01 (B) WITHDRAWS FROM MEMBERSHIP IN THE 02 FEDERAL SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM]. 03 * Sec. 2. AS 39.30.180(3) is amended to read: 04 (3) "participating employer" means 05 [(A) THE STATE OF ALASKA; AND 06 (B)] an employer, 07 [(i) WHO IS AN EMPLOYER] as defined in 08 AS 14.25.220 or AS 39.35.680, that does not participate [; 09 (ii) WHO HAS NEVER PARTICIPATED IN OR HAS 10 WITHDRAWN FROM PARTICIPATION] in the federal social 11 security system [; AND 12 (iii) WHOSE PARTICIPATION IN THE 13 SUPPLEMENTAL EMPLOYEE BENEFIT PROGRAM HAS BEEN 14 APPROVED BY THE COMMISSIONER]. 15 * Sec. 3.
