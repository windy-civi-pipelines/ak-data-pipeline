AS 08.18.071(b) is amended to read: 04 (b) The amount of the bond for a 05 (1) general contractor shall be $25,000; 06 (2) general contractor with a residential contractor endorsement under 07 AS 08.18.025 who performs exclusively residential work shall be $20,000; 08 (3) mechanical or specialty contractor or home inspector shall be 09 $10,000; or 10 (4) contractor whose work on one project with an aggregate contract 11 price of $20,000 [$10,000] or less, including all labor, materials, and other items, 12 when the work is not part of a larger or major operation or otherwise divided into 13 contracts of less than $20,000 [$10,000] to evade a higher bonding requirement, shall 14 be $10,000 [$5,000].
