AS 31.25.005 is amended to read: 06 Sec. 31.25.005. Purpose. The corporation shall, for the benefit of the state, to 07 the fullest extent possible, 08 (1) develop and have primary responsibility for developing natural gas 09 pipelines, an Alaska liquefied natural gas project, and other transportation mechanisms 10 to deliver natural gas in-state for the maximum benefit of the people of the state; 11 (2) when developing natural gas pipelines, an Alaska liquefied natural 12 gas project, and other transportation mechanisms to deliver natural gas in-state, 13 provide economic benefits in the state and revenue to the state; 14 (3) assist the Department of Natural Resources and the Department of 01 Revenue to maximize the value of the state's royalty natural gas, natural gas delivered 02 to the state as payment of tax, and other natural gas received by the state; 03 (4) advance an in-state natural gas pipeline as described in the July 1, 04 2011, project plan prepared under former AS 38.34.040 by the corporation while a 05 subsidiary of the Alaska Housing Finance Corporation, with modifications determined 06 by the corporation to be appropriate to develop, finance, construct, and operate an in- 07 state natural gas pipeline in a safe, prudent, economical, and efficient manner, for the 08 purpose of making natural gas, including propane and other hydrocarbons associated 09 with natural gas other than oil, available to Fairbanks, the Southcentral region of the 10 state, and other communities in the state at the lowest rates possible; an in-state 11 natural gas pipeline advanced under this paragraph must include a direct spur 12 line to the City of Fairbanks and the Fairbanks North Star Borough; 13 (5) advance an Alaska liquefied natural gas project by developing 14 infrastructure and providing related services, including services related to 15 transportation, liquefaction, a marine terminal, marketing, and commercial support; if 16 the corporation provides a service under this paragraph to the state, a public 17 corporation or instrumentality of the state, a political subdivision of the state, or 18 another entity of the state, the corporation may not charge a fee for the service in an 19 amount greater than the amount necessary to reimburse the corporation for the cost of 20 the service; an in-state natural gas pipeline advanced under this paragraph must 21 include a direct spur line to the City of Fairbanks and the Fairbanks North Star 22 Borough; 23 (6) endeavor to develop natural gas pipelines and other transportation 24 mechanisms to deliver natural gas, including propane and other hydrocarbons 25 associated with natural gas other than oil, to public utility and industrial customers in 26 areas of the state to which the natural gas, including propane and other hydrocarbons 27 associated with natural gas other than oil, may be delivered at commercially 28 reasonable rates; and 29 (7) endeavor to develop natural gas pipelines and other transportation 30 mechanisms that offer commercially reasonable rates for shippers and access for 31 shippers who produce natural gas, including propane and other hydrocarbons 01 associated with natural gas other than oil, in the state. 02 * Sec. 2. AS 43.55.011 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 03 (q) The department shall separately account for the amount of tax levied under 04 this section from gas that was transported through a pipeline or other infrastructure 05 developed under AS 31.25.005(4) or (5). The legislature may appropriate the amount 06 accounted for under this section to construct natural gas line connections to places of 07 business and residence. 08 * Sec. 3. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
