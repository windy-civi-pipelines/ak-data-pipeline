The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 08 to read: 09 LEGISLATIVE INTENT. It is the intent of the legislature to preserve the right of 10 women to deliver children at home with licensed midwives, to remove obstacles for safe 11 deliveries outside of hospitals, to ensure quality care of pregnant women and the children they 12 deliver, and to guarantee that the practice of midwifery by a licensed midwife is covered by 13 insurance regardless of the location where the care is received. 01 * Sec. 2.
