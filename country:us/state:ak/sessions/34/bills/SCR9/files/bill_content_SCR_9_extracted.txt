relating to the powers, duties, and liability of emergency medical technicians and mobile intensive care paramedics; and relating to the practice of veterinary medicine. 00 SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 9 01 Suspending Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State 02 Legislature, concerning House Bill No. 70, relating to emergency medical services for 03 operational canines; relating to the powers, duties, and liability of emergency medical 04 technicians and mobile intensive care paramedics; and relating to the practice of 05 veterinary medicine. 06 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 07 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 08 Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, regarding 09 changes to the title of a bill, are suspended in consideration of House Bill No. 70, relating to 10 emergency medical services for operational canines; relating to the powers, duties, and 11 liability of emergency medical technicians and mobile intensive care paramedics; and relating 12 to the practice of veterinary medicine.
