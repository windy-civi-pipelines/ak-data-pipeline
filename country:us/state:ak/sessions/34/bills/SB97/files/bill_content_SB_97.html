SB 97: "An Act establishing a big game guide concession area permit program on land in the state; relating to the duties of the Big Game Commercial Services Board, the Board of Game, the Department of Fish and Game, and the Department of Natural Resources; requiring the Board of Game to establish an initial big game guide concession area; and providing for an effective date." 

00                            SENATE BILL NO. 97                                                                          
01 "An Act establishing a big game guide concession area permit program on land in the                                     
02 state; relating to the duties of the Big Game Commercial Services Board, the Board of                                   
03 Game, the Department of Fish and Game, and the Department of Natural Resources;                                         
04 requiring the Board of Game to establish an initial big game guide concession area; and                                 
05 providing for an effective date."                                                                                       
06 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
07    * Section 1. AS 08.54.600(a) is amended by adding a new paragraph to read:                                         
08                 (10)  coordinate and consult with the Board of Game and the                                             
09       Department of Natural Resources to fulfill the duties of each under AS 16.05.263 and                              
10       AS 38.05.021.                                                                                                     
11    * Sec. 2. AS 16.05 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                     
12            Sec. 16.05.263. Establishment of big game guide concession areas. (a) The                                  
13       Board of Game shall consider and approve or disapprove a proposal to establish a big                              



01 game guide concession area that would be administered by the Department of Natural                                      
02 Resources under AS 38.05.021 in a single game management unit or subunit. The                                           
03 board may only establish a big game guide concession area by approving a proposal                                       
04 submitted under this section to do so. A big game guide may conduct a hunt in a                                         
05 concession area established under this section only if the guide holds a big game guide                                 
06       concession area permit issued under AS 38.05.021.                                                                 
07 (b)  A person may submit a proposal to the Board of Game for the                                                        
08 establishment of a big game guide concession area. After providing 15 days' public                                      
09 notice, the board may consider the proposal at the board's next regular meeting in the                                  
10       region where the big game guide concession area is proposed.                                                      
11 (c)  After taking public comment, the Board of Game, in consultation with the                                           
12 Department of Fish and Game, the Department of Natural Resources, and the Big                                           
13 Game Commercial Services Board established under AS 08.54.591, may approve a                                            
14 proposal to establish a big game guide concession area if the board determines that                                     
15       establishing the area                                                                                             
16 (1)  supports the conservation and management of the state's land and                                                   
17       big game resources;                                                                                               
18 (2)  aids in the enforcement of state statutes and regulations relating to                                              
19       guided hunts for big game; and                                                                                    
20                 (3)  is in the public interest.                                                                         
21 (d)  If the Board of Game approves the establishment of a big game guide                                                
22 concession area under this section, the board shall determine the number of full big                                    
23 game guide concession area permits and limited big game guide concession area                                           
24 permits that the Department of Natural Resources shall make available under                                             
25 AS 38.05.021. To determine the number of full big game guide concession area                                            
26 permits and limited big game guide concession area permits that should be issued for a                                  
27 big game guide concession area and the specific authorizations that should attach to                                    
28       each concession permit authorized for the area, the Board of Game                                                 
29 (1)  may establish advisory boards composed of representatives of the                                                   
30 state departments and boards listed in (c) of this section who are familiar with the area                               
31       and interested members of the public to assist the board in making the determination;                             



01                 (2)  may not                                                                                            
02                      (A)  combine more than three existing guide use areas into a                                       
03            single big game guide concession area;                                                                       
04                      (B)  establish more than one big game guide concession area in                                     
05            a single guide use area.                                                                                     
06            (e)  In this section,                                                                                        
07                 (1)  "full big game guide concession area permit" has the meaning                                       
08       given in AS 38.05.021(j);                                                                                         
09                 (2)  "game management unit" has the meaning given in AS 08.54.790;                                      
10                 (3)  "guide use area" has the meaning given in AS 08.54.750(g);                                         
11 (4)  "limited big game guide concession area permit" has the meaning                                                    
12       given in AS 38.05.021(j).                                                                                         
13    * Sec. 3. AS 38.05 is amended by adding a new section to read:                                                     
14 Sec. 38.05.021. Big game guide concession area permit program. (a) The                                                
15 commissioner shall implement a big game guide concession area permit program to                                         
16 limit the number of individuals authorized to conduct big game guiding on land in the                                   
17 state where the Board of Game has approved the establishment of a big game guide                                        
18       concession area under AS 16.05.263.                                                                               
19 (b)  The commissioner shall administer the big game guide concession area                                               
20       permit program to                                                                                                 
21                 (1)  encourage conservation of the state's land and wildlife resources;                                 
22 (2)  provide a superior hunting experience to individuals who retain big                                                
23 game guiding services by limiting the number of guided hunts occurring in the same                                      
24       area;                                                                                                             
25 (3)  reduce user conflicts between big game guides and between big                                                      
26       game guides and other users of land in the state;                                                                 
27 (4)  ensure a responsible, professional, and economically healthy big                                                   
28       game guide guiding industry in the state; and                                                                     
29 (5)  incentivize long-term planning and the conservation of natural                                                     
30       resources by big game guides.                                                                                     
31 (c)  The department shall issue full big game guide concession area and limited                                         



01 big game guide concession area permits for a big game guide concession area                                             
02 established by the Board of Game under AS 16.05.263 in an open, public, and                                             
03 competitive process. The department shall establish permit standards and scoring                                        
04 criteria, but may not provide that a permit be issued based on the highest bid amount.                                  
05 A big game guide may not hold more than three concession permits at a time for state                                    
06 land issued under this subsection and is subject to the requirements of AS 08.54.750.                                   
07 A concession permit issued under this subsection is valid for 10 years from the date                                    
08 issued. A concession permit issued under this subsection may not be extended or                                         
09 renewed without the same open and competitive process provided by the department                                        
10       for the issuance of a concession permit under this subsection.                                                    
11 (d)  The commissioner, in consultation with the Big Game Commercial                                                     
12 Services Board established under AS 08.54.591, the Board of Game, and the                                               
13 Department of Fish and Game, shall adopt regulations necessary to establish and                                         
14 administer the big game guide concession area permit program, including regulations                                     
15       relating to                                                                                                       
16 (1)  the qualifications for a full big game guide concession area permit                                                
17       or a limited big game guide concession area permit;                                                               
18 (2)  the process for issuing full big game guide concession area permits                                                
19 and limited big game guide concession area permits and the scope and authorizations                                     
20       provided by each permit;                                                                                          
21 (3)  the determination and collection of reasonable concession permit                                                   
22 fees; in determining reasonable concession permit fees, the commissioner shall                                          
23       consider the following:                                                                                           
24 (A)  fees paid by big game guides holding similar concession                                                            
25            permits in other jurisdictions;                                                                              
26 (B)  the number and type of concession permits issued for a big                                                         
27            game guide concession area.                                                                                  
28 (e)  Subject to the approval of the commissioner, a big game guide who holds a                                          
29 concession permit under this section may, during the term of the permit, transfer the                                   
30 concession permit to another individual qualified under regulations adopted under this                                  
31 section. In approving or disapproving the transfer of a permit, the commissioner shall                                  



01 consider the circumstances of the proposed transfer and whether the transfer will                                       
02       promote the principles listed in (b) of this section.                                                             
03 (f)  The commissioner, in consultation with the Big Game Commercial                                                     
04 Services Board established under AS 08.54.591, the Board of Game, and the                                               
05 Department of Fish and Game, may suspend or revoke a concession permit issued                                           
06 under this section. If a permittee violates the terms of a concession permit issued under                               
07 this section, the commissioner may suspend or revoke the concession permit after                                        
08       providing the permittee with written notice and an opportunity to be heard.                                       
09 (g)  The commissioner shall keep confidential information provided by the Big                                           
10 Game Commercial Services Board under AS 08.54.680 or 08.54.760 and any                                                  
11 propriety information, confidential commercial information, or confidential financial                                   
12 information used by the department in the course of approving or denying an                                             
13       application for a big game guide concession area permit offered under this section.                               
14 (h)  The commissioner is responsible for administration and enforcement of                                              
15 this section and may delegate that authority as appropriate. The department may issue                                   
16       a citation for a violation of this section or a regulation adopted under this section.                            
17 (i)  In issuing a full big game guide concession area permit or limited big game                                        
18 guide concession area permit under this section, the commissioner, in consultation                                      
19 with the Big Game Commercial Services Board established under AS 08.54.591, the                                         
20 Board of Game, the Department of Fish and Game, and private landowners who own                                          
21 5,000 or more acres of land in the game management unit where a concession program                                      
22 is established shall consider each applicant's professional history and reputation, in                                  
23       addition to other applicable criteria in the process required by (c) of this section.                             
24            (j)  In this section,                                                                                        
25 (1)  "concession permit" means a permit granting a big game guide the                                                   
26 right to conduct commercial big game hunts on land within a geographic area defined                                     
27       by the department;                                                                                                
28 (2)  "full big game guide concession area permit" means a concession                                                    
29 permit made available by the department through a competitive process that limits the                                   
30 number of clients that a permit holder may provide big game guided hunts to in the                                      
31 area and limits the species the permit holder may provide guided hunts for in the                                       



01       concession area;                                                                                                  
02 (3)  "limited big game guide concession area permit" means a                                                            
03 concession permit made available by the department through a random draw                                                
04 application process that provides more restrictive limitations than a full big game                                     
05 guide concession area permit on the number of clients that a permit holder may                                          
06 provide big game guided hunts to in the area and limits the species the permit holder                                   
07       may provide guided hunts for in the concession area.                                                              
08    * Sec. 4. AS 08.54.600(a)(9); AS 16.05.262; and AS 38.05.022 are repealed.                                         
09    * Sec. 5. Sections 46 and 48, ch. 15, SLA 2024, are repealed.                                                      
10    * Sec. 6. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to                          
11 read:                                                                                                                   
12 INITIAL BIG GAME GUIDE CONCESSION AREA AND PERMIT PROGRAM.                                                              
13 (a) Notwithstanding AS 16.05.263, added by sec. 2 of this Act, the Board of Game shall                                  
14 establish the initial big game guide concession area under this section. In establishing the                            
15 initial concession area under this section, the Board of Game, after taking public comment                              
16 and in consultation with the Big Game Commercial Services Board established under                                       
17 AS 08.54.591, the Department of Fish and Game, and the Department of Natural Resources,                                 
18 shall identify and select a single game management unit or subunit that the Board of Game                               
19 determines would benefit most from the establishment of a big game guide concession area                                
20 permit program under AS 38.05.021, added by sec. 3 of this Act, in consideration of the                                 
21 principles of AS 38.05.021(b), added by sec. 3 of this Act. The Board of Game shall                                     
22 determine the number of full big game guide concession area permits and limited big game                                
23 guide concession area permits that the Department of Natural Resources shall make available                             
24 in the initial big game guide concession area established under this section and the specific                           
25 authorizations that should attach to each concession permit authorized under this subsection                            
26 for the area using the process provided in AS 16.05.263(d), added by sec. 2 of this Act.                                
27 (b)  Notwithstanding the requirement in AS 38.05.021(a) and (c), added by sec. 3 of                                     
28 this Act, that the Board of Game approve the establishment of the area under AS 16.05.263,                              
29 added by sec. 2 of this Act, the Department of Natural Resources shall establish and                                    
30 administer a big game guide concession area permit program under AS 38.05.021, added by                                 
31 sec. 3 of this Act, in the game management unit or subunit selected by the Board of Game                                



01 under (a) of this section.                                                                                              
02       (c)  In this section,                                                                                             
03            (1)  "concession permit" means a permit granting a big game guide the right to                               
04 conduct commercial big game hunts on land within the boundaries of the region selected by                               
05 the Board of Game under (a) of this section;                                                                            
06            (2)  "full big game guide concession area permit" has the meaning given in                                   
07 AS 38.05.021(j);                                                                                                        
08            (3)  "game management unit" has the meaning given in AS 08.54.790;                                           
09            (4)  "limited big game guide concession area permit" has the meaning given in                                
10 AS 38.05.021(j).                                                                                                        
11    * Sec. 7. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to                          
12 read:                                                                                                                   
13 TRANSITION. Notwithstanding the effective date of AS 16.05.263, added by sec. 2                                         
14 of this Act, the Board of Game may only accept and consider a proposal to establish a big                               
15 game guide concession area under AS 16.05.263, added by sec. 2 of this Act, if                                          
16 (1)  the Board of Game has established the concession area required under sec.                                          
17 2 of this Act; and                                                                                                      
18 (2)  the Department of Natural Resources has implemented and administered a                                             
19 big game guide concession area permit program in the concession area established under sec.                             
20 2 of this Act for a period of at least three years, notwithstanding the requirement in                                  
21 AS 38.05.021(a) and (c), added by sec. 3 of this Act, that the Board of Game approve the                                
22 establishment of the area under AS 16.05.263, added by sec. 2 of this Act.                                              
23    * Sec. 8. This Act is retroactive to July 23, 2024.                                                                
24    * Sec. 9. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
