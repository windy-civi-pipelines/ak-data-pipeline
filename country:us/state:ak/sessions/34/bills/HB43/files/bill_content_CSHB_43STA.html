CSHB 43(STA): "An Act relating to elections; relating to voters; relating to voter registration; relating to election administration; relating to the crime of unlawful interference with voting; relating to campaign signs; relating to the Alaska Public Offices Commission; relating to public official financial disclosures; and providing for an effective date."
