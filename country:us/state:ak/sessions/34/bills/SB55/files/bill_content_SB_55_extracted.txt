AS 14.25.070(a) is amended to read: 05 (a) Each employer shall contribute to the system every payroll period an 06 amount calculated by applying a rate of 14.56 [12.56] percent to the total of all base 07 salaries paid by the employer to active members of the system and to members who 08 are retired from the plan and reemployed under AS 14.20.136, including any 09 adjustments to contributions required by AS 14.25.173(a). 10 * Sec. 2. AS 14.25.350(a) is amended to read: 11 (a) An employer shall contribute to each member's individual account an 12 amount equal to nine [SEVEN] percent of the member's compensation from July 1 to 13 the following June 30. 14 * Sec. 3. AS 39.30.170(a) is amended to read: 01 (a) An employer in either the teachers' retirement system (AS 14.25) or the 02 public employees' retirement system (AS 39.35) that does not participate in the 03 federal social security system shall [MAY] become a participating employer in the 04 employee benefits program under AS 39.30.150 - 39.30.180 [, IF 05 (1) THE EMPLOYER PARTICIPATES AS AN EMPLOYER IN 06 THE PUBLIC EMPLOYEES' RETIREMENT SYSTEM UNDER AS 39.35; AND 07 (2) THE EMPLOYER 08 (A) IS ELIGIBLE FOR MEMBERSHIP IN BUT HAS 09 NEVER ELECTED TO BECOME A MEMBER OF THE FEDERAL 10 SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM; OR 11 (B) WITHDRAWS FROM MEMBERSHIP IN THE 12 FEDERAL SOCIAL SECURITY SYSTEM]. 13 * Sec. 4. AS 39.30.180(3) is amended to read: 14 (3) "participating employer" means 15 (A) the State of Alaska; [AND] 16 (B) an employer who 17 (i) [WHO] is an employer as defined in AS 14.25.220, 18 14.25.590, AS 39.35.680, or 39.35.990; and 19 (ii) does not participate [WHO HAS NEVER 20 PARTICIPATED IN OR HAS WITHDRAWN FROM 21 PARTICIPATION] in the federal social security system [; AND 22 (iii) WHOSE PARTICIPATION IN THE 23 SUPPLEMENTAL EMPLOYEE BENEFIT PROGRAM HAS BEEN 24 APPROVED BY THE COMMISSIONER]. 25 * Sec. 5.
