AS 18.80.240 is amended by adding new subsections to read: 06 (b) It is unlawful for the owner, lessee, manager, or other person having the 07 right to lease or rent residential real property 08 (1) to refuse to lease or rent the real property to a person because of the 09 person's medical debt; 10 (2) to discriminate against a person in a term, condition, or privilege 11 relating to the use, lease, or rental of the real property because of the person's medical 12 debt; 13 (3) to make a written or oral inquiry or record of the medical debt of a 14 person seeking to rent or lease the real property; 01 (4) to represent to a person that the real property is not available for 02 inspection, rental, or lease when in fact it is so available, or to refuse to allow a person 03 to inspect the real property because of the person's medical debt; 04 (5) to make, print, or publish, or cause to be made, printed, or 05 published, any notice, statement, or advertisement with respect to the rental of the real 06 property that indicates any preference, limitation, or discrimination based on a 07 person's medical debt. 08 (c) In this section, "medical debt" has the meaning given in AS 45.48.800(c). 09 * Sec. 2. AS 45.48 is amended by adding a new section to read: 10 Article 6A. Miscellaneous Provisions. 11 Sec. 45.48.800. Medical debt. (a) A medical creditor or medical debt collector 12 may not communicate with or report information to a consumer credit reporting 13 agency regarding a medical debt. If a medical creditor or medical debt collector 14 communicates with or reports information to a consumer credit reporting agency 15 regarding the medical debt of a consumer in violation of this subsection, the 16 consumer's agreement to pay the debt is void and unenforceable. 17 (b) A consumer credit reporting agency may not include medical debt in a 18 consumer report or use medical debt to calculate a credit score. 19 (c) In this section, 20 (1) "medical creditor" means an entity that provides health care 21 services and to whom a consumer owes or, if purchased by a medical debt collector, 22 owed medical debt; 23 (2) "medical debt" means an obligation for the payment of money 24 arising out of an agreement or contract, express or implied, for the provision of health 25 care services, products, or devices; "medical debt" does not include debt charged to a 26 credit card or an extension of credit made by a financial institution to a borrower; 27 (3) "medical debt collector" means a person that 28 (A) regularly collects or attempts to collect, directly or 29 indirectly, medical debt originally owed or due, or asserted to be owed or due, 30 to another; 31 (B) purchases medical debt from an entity that provides health 01 care services. 02 * Sec. 3. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
