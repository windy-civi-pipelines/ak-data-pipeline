AS 14.30 is amended by adding a new section to article 10 to read: 05 Sec. 14.30.430. Education in history and contributions of Asian Americans 06 and Pacific Islanders. (a) Each school district shall establish and provide to students 07 in kindergarten through grade 12 a program, approved by the department, that includes 08 (1) instruction on the history of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders 09 and contributions made by Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders in the United States; 10 and 11 (2) instructional materials that portray the cultural and economic 12 diversity of Asian Americans and Pacific Islanders. 13 (b) In this section, "school district" has the meaning given in AS 14.30.350.
