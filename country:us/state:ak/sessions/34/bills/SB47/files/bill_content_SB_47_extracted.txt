AS 41.21.122 is amended to read: 04 Sec. 41.21.122. Management responsibility. Except as provided by (b) and 05 (c) of this section, control, development, and maintenance of [THE] state land and 06 water described in AS 41.21.121 as the Chugach State Park and control of highway 07 access and roadside structures within the park is assigned to the department [FOR 08 CONTROL, DEVELOPMENT AND MAINTENANCE, EXCEPT THAT THE 09 DEPARTMENT OF TRANSPORTATION AND PUBLIC FACILITIES IS 10 RESPONSIBLE FOR THE REPAIR AND MAINTENANCE OF ALL EXISTING 11 PUBLIC ROADS WITHIN THE PARK. THE CONTROL OF HIGHWAY ACCESS 12 AND ROADSIDE STRUCTURES WITHIN THE CHUGACH STATE PARK IS 13 THE RESPONSIBILITY OF THE DEPARTMENT]. The Department of Fish and 14 Game shall cooperate with the department to provide for those purposes under 15 AS 41.21.121 relevant to the duties of the Department of Fish and Game. If, in the 01 exercise of management responsibility under this section, conflicts of land ownership, 02 jurisdiction, or authority are unreconcilable or unmanageable other than at an 03 unreasonable cost and expense to the state, the commissioner, after public hearing in 04 accordance with AS 44.62, may modify, subject to the approval of the legislature, the 05 boundaries of the Chugach State Park, not to exceed 20 percent of the total acreage 06 described in AS 41.21.121. 07 * Sec. 2. AS 41.21.122 is amended by adding new subsections to read: 08 (b) The department may grant an easement or right-of-way within the 09 Chugach State Park to a municipality if 10 (1) the department provides notice of the proposed grant in the manner 11 provided by AS 44.62.190 and 44.62.200 and conducts at least one hearing in 12 accordance with the provisions of AS 44.62.210; and 13 (2) the commissioner finds that granting the easement or right-of-way 14 (A) will improve, facilitate, or maintain public access to the 15 park; and 16 (B) is in the public interest. 17 (c) The Department of Transportation and Public Facilities shall repair and 18 maintain existing public roads within the Chugach State Park except for repairs and 19 maintenance performed by a municipality under (b) of this section. 20 * Sec. 3.
