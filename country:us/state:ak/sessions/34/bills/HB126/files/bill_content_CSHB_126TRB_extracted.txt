AS 10.06.960(k) is amended to read: 05 (k) Notwithstanding (i) of this section and the requirement provided under 06 AS 10.06.633(e) that a corporation may only be reinstated within two years from 07 the date of the certificate of involuntary dissolution, a corporation that is organized 08 as a Native village corporation under the Act and [,] that has been involuntarily 09 dissolved by the commissioner under AS 10.06.633 [, AND THAT HAS FAILED TO 10 APPLY FOR REINSTATEMENT DURING THE PERIOD ESTABLISHED UNDER 11 AS 10.06.633(e)] may be reinstated under AS 10.06.633(e) at any time [ON OR 12 BEFORE DECEMBER 31, 2020]. The reinstated corporation and its shareholders 13 have all of the rights, privileges, liabilities, and obligations that would have applied to 14 them if the corporation had not been dissolved, and all corporate and shareholder 01 actions taken during the period of dissolution are considered to be as valid as if 02 dissolution had not occurred. If a corporation elects to reinstate under this subsection 03 and if the corporation's previously used corporate name is no longer available for use 04 by the corporation, then, notwithstanding AS 10.06.502 - 10.06.510, an amendment to 05 the articles of incorporation changing the previously used corporate name may be 06 adopted by action of the corporation's board of directors alone. 07 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
