II, sec. 10, Constitution of the State of Alaska, provides that neither 06 house of the legislature may adjourn or recess for longer than three days unless the other 07 house concurs; and 08 WHEREAS Rule 52, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, provides that 09 adoption of a concurrent resolution by a majority vote of the full membership of each house 10 constitutes concurrence; 11 BE IT RESOLVED by the Alaska State Legislature that the Senate and the House of 12 Representatives may each recess for a period of more than three days during the First Special 13 Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature.
