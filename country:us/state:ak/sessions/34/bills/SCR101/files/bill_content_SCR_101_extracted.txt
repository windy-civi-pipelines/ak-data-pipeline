II, sec. 10, Constitution of the State of Alaska, provides that neither 05 house of the legislature may adjourn or recess for longer than three days unless the other 06 house concurs; and 07 WHEREAS Rule 52, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, provides that 08 adoption of a concurrent resolution by a majority vote of the full membership of each house 09 constitutes concurrence; 10 BE IT RESOLVED by the Alaska State Legislature that the Senate and the House of 11 Representatives may each recess for a period of more than three days during the First Special 12 Session of the Thirty-Fourth Alaska State Legislature.
