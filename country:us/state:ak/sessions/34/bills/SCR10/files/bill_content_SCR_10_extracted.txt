relating to the care of children in state custody placed in residential facilities outside the state; and amending Rule 12.1(b), Alaska Child in Need of Aid Rules of Procedure. 00 SENATE CONCURRENT RESOLUTION NO. 10 01 Suspending Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State 02 Legislature, concerning House Bill No. 36, relating to the placement of foster children in 03 psychiatric hospitals; relating to the care of children in state custody placed in 04 residential facilities outside the state; and amending Rule 12.1(b), Alaska Child in Need 05 of Aid Rules of Procedure. 06 BE IT RESOLVED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA: 07 That under Rule 54, Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, the provisions of 08 Rules 24(c), 35, 41(b), and 42(e), Uniform Rules of the Alaska State Legislature, regarding 09 changes to the title of a bill, are suspended in consideration of House Bill No. 36, relating to 10 the placement of foster children in psychiatric hospitals; relating to the care of children in 11 state custody placed in residential facilities outside the state; and amending Rule 12.1(b), 12 Alaska Child in Need of Aid Rules of Procedure.
