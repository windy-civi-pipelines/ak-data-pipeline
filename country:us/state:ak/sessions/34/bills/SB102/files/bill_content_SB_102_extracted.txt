AS 44.12 is amended by adding a new section to read: 05 Article 5. Standard Time. 06 Sec. 44.12.400. Exemption from daylight saving time. The state exempts 07 itself from observation of advanced time, also known as daylight saving time, during 08 the period set under 15 U.S.C. 260a for the advancement of time each calendar year, 09 and the entire state shall observe the standard time that is otherwise applicable during 10 that period. 11 * Sec. 2.
