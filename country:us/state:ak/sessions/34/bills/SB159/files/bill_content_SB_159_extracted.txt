AS 23.15.630(a) is amended to read: 05 (a) In the manner provided in AS 23.20, the department shall collect from 06 each employee an amount equal to .20 [ONE-TENTH OF ONE] percent of the wages, 07 as set out in AS 23.20.175, on which the employee is required to make contributions 08 under AS 23.20.290(d).
