The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section 05 to read: 06 DISAPPROVAL OF THE STATE OFFICERS COMPENSATION COMMISSION'S 07 RECOMMENDATIONS. All recommendations contained in the 2025 final report of the State 08 Officers Compensation Commission are disapproved. 09 * Sec. 2. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
