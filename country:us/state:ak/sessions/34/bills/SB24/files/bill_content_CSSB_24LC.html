04 Sec. 43.50.885. Restrictions on electronic smoking products. A person may                                             
05 sell or distribute to consumers in this state, acquire, hold, own, possess, or transport                                
06 for sale or distribution in this state, or import or cause to be imported into this state for

18       ingredients and nicotine content.
