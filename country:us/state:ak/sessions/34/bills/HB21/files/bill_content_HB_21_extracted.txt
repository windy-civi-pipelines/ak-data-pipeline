AS 15.07.040 is amended by adding new subsections to read: 06 (b) A person who is at least 16 years of age and under 18 years of age may 07 preregister to vote. To preregister, a person shall supply the information required 08 under AS 15.07.060(a) to a registration official or a voter registration agency, except 09 that, instead of a declaration that the applicant will be 18 years of age or older within 10 90 days after the date of registration under AS 15.07.060(a)(6), the person shall supply 11 a declaration that the person will be 18 years of age or older within two years after the 12 date of preregistration. 13 (c) The division shall register a person preregistered to vote under (b) of this 14 section 90 days before the person reaches 18 years of age and forward to the person a 01 registration card. 02 * Sec. 2. AS 15.07.195 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 03 (e) Notwithstanding another provision of this section, the address and 04 telephone number of a person under 18 years of age who registers or preregisters to 05 vote are confidential and not open to public inspection.
