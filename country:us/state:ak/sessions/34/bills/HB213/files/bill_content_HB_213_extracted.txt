AS 28.05.068(a) is amended to read: 05 (a) If the department conveys, distributes, or communicates data to be used in 06 a database, index, pointer system, or any other system managed by an entity other than 07 the department [, INCLUDING THE AMERICAN ASSOCIATION OF MOTOR 08 VEHICLE ADMINISTRATORS,] to comply with the requirements of P.L. 109-13, 09 Division B (REAL ID Act of 2005), the department may not convey, distribute, or 10 communicate to that entity any data not required to be conveyed, distributed, or 11 communicated for the state to be certified by the United States Department of 12 Homeland Security to be in compliance with the requirements of P.L. 109-13, 13 Division B (REAL ID Act of 2005). 14 * Sec. 2. AS 28.05.068(b) is amended to read: 01 (b) Notwithstanding (a) of this section, the department may [NOT] convey, 02 distribute, or communicate data to a private entity only [ANY SCANNED OR 03 STORED DOCUMENTS COLLECTED] in order to carry out the provisions of 04 AS 28.15.151(g) [AS 28.15]. 05 * Sec. 3. AS 28.15.151 is amended by adding a new subsection to read: 06 (g) The department shall, upon request, furnish information maintained by the 07 department under this section to an entity approved by the United States Social 08 Security Administration to facilitate driver's license data verification. 09 * Sec. 4. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
