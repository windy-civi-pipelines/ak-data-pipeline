AS 14.33 is amended by adding a new section to read: 05 Article 5. Wireless Telecommunications Devices. 06 Sec. 14.33.300. Wireless telecommunications device policy. (a) The 07 department shall develop and adopt a model policy that prohibits the use of nonschool- 08 issued wireless telecommunications devices in public schools during regular school 09 hours, including lunch and passing periods. The policy must allow exceptions for 10 students to use a wireless telecommunications device for medical purposes, in the 11 event of an emergency, or when a teacher or administrator of the school grants 12 permission to a student to use a wireless telecommunications device for educational 13 purposes. 14 (b) Each school district shall adopt a policy that prohibits students from 01 possessing or using a nonschool-issued wireless telecommunications device during 02 regular school hours, including lunch and passing periods. Each school district shall 03 share this policy with parents or guardians, students, volunteers, and school 04 employees. The policy must allow exceptions for students to use a wireless 05 telecommunications device for medical purposes, in the event of an emergency, or 06 when a teacher or administrator of the school grants permission to a student to use a 07 wireless telecommunications device for educational purposes. A school district may 08 either develop and adopt its own policy or adopt the model policy developed under (a) 09 of this section. 10 (c) This section does not authorize a person to monitor, collect, or access 11 information related to a student's use of a wireless telecommunications device. 12 (d) In this section, "wireless telecommunications device" means any portable 13 wireless device that has the capability to provide voice, messaging, or other data 14 communication between two or more parties. 15 * Sec. 2.
