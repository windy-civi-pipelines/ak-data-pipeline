HB 37: "An Act relating to the posting of warning signs for alcoholic beverages." 

00                             HOUSE BILL NO. 37                                                                          
01 "An Act relating to the posting of warning signs for alcoholic beverages."                                              
02 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
03    * Section 1. AS 04.21.065(a) is amended to read:                                                                   
04            (a)  A holder of one of the following types of licenses or permits shall post on                             
05       the licensed or designated premises two [THREE] separate warning signs as described                           
06       in (b) of this section:                                                                                           
07                 (1)  brewery manufacturer license under AS 04.09.020 with a                                             
08       manufacturer sampling endorsement under AS 04.09.410;                                                             
09                 (2)  winery manufacturer license under AS 04.09.030 with a                                              
10       manufacturer sampling endorsement under AS 04.09.410;                                                             
11                 (3)  distillery manufacturer license under AS 04.09.040 with a                                          
12       manufacturer sampling endorsement under AS 04.09.410;                                                             
13                 (4)  beverage dispensary license under AS 04.09.200;                                                    
14                 (5)  restaurant or eating place license under AS 04.09.210;                                             
15                 (6)  club license under AS 04.09.220;                                                                   



01                 (7)  package store license under AS 04.09.230;                                                          
02                 (8)  pub license under AS 04.09.240;                                                                    
03                 (9)  theater license under AS 04.09.250;                                                                
04                 (10)  common carrier dispensary license under AS 04.09.260;                                             
05                 (11)  sporting activity or event license under AS 04.09.270;                                            
06                 (12)  outdoor recreation lodge license under AS 04.09.280;                                              
07                 (13)  fair license under AS 04.09.290;                                                                  
08                 (14)  golf course license under AS 04.09.300;                                                           
09                 (15)  destination resort license under AS 04.09.310;                                                    
10                 (16)  brewery retail license under AS 04.09.320;                                                        
11                 (17)  winery retail license under AS 04.09.330;                                                         
12                 (18)  distillery retail license under AS 04.09.340;                                                     
13                 (19)  beverage dispensary tourism license under AS 04.09.350;                                           
14 (20)  seasonal restaurant or eating place tourism license under                                                         
15       AS 04.09.360;                                                                                                     
16                 (21)  beverage dispensary caterer's permit under AS 04.09.610;                                          
17                 (22)  restaurant caterer's dining permit under AS 04.09.620;                                            
18                 (23)  club caterer's permit under AS 04.09.630;                                                         
19                 (24)  art exhibit event permit under AS 04.09.640;                                                      
20                 (25)  music festival permit under AS 04.09.650;                                                         
21                 (26)  nonprofit organization event permit under AS 04.09.660;                                           
22                 (27)  package store tasting event permit under AS 04.09.680;                                            
23                 (28)  conditional contractor's permit under AS 04.09.710;                                               
24 (29)  another license or permit issued by the board authorizing                                                         
25       consumption of alcoholic beverages.                                                                             
26    * Sec. 2. AS 04.21.065(b) is amended to read:                                                                      
27 (b)  The warning signs required by (a) of this section must be at least 11 inches                                       
28 by 14 inches, and the lettering must be at least one-half inch high and in contrasting                                  
29 colors. The first sign must read, "WARNING: Alcohol use [DRINKING                                                   
30 ALCOHOLIC BEVERAGES SUCH AS BEER, WINE, WINE COOLERS, AND                                                               
31 DISTILLED SPIRITS OR SMOKING CIGARETTES] during pregnancy can cause                                                     



01 birth defects. Alcohol use can cause cancer, including breast and colon cancers."                                   
02 The second sign must read, "WARNING: An unaccompanied person under 21                                               
03 years of age who enters these premises in violation of law may, under                                               
04 AS 04.16.049(e), be civilly liable for damages of $1,500. A person who provides                                     
05 alcoholic beverages to a person under 21 years of age, if convicted under                                               
06 AS 04.16.051, could be imprisoned for up to five years and fined up to $50,000."                                        
07 [THE THIRD SIGN MUST READ, "WARNING: AN UNACCOMPANIED                                                                   
08 PERSON UNDER 21 YEARS OF AGE WHO ENTERS THESE PREMISES IN                                                               
09 VIOLATION OF LAW MAY, UNDER AS 04.16.049(e), BE CIVILLY LIABLE                                                          
10 FOR DAMAGES OF $1,500."] The license or permit holder shall display the first                                           
11 sign [AND SECOND SIGNS] in a manner that would make it [THEM] conspicuous                                       
12 to a person who will be purchasing or consuming alcoholic beverages or smoking                                          
13 cigarettes on the licensed or designated premises and shall conspicuously display the                                   
14 second [THIRD] sign at each door through which customers enter the licensed                                         
15       premises.
