SB 35: "An Act relating to transportation network and delivery network companies." 

00                            SENATE BILL NO. 35                                                                          
01 "An Act relating to transportation network and delivery network companies."                                             
02 BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF ALASKA:                                                                
03    * Section 1. AS 23.30.230(a) is amended to read:                                                                 
04            (a)  The following persons are not covered by this chapter:                                                  
05                 (1)  a part-time babysitter;                                                                            
06                 (2)  a cleaning person;                                                                                 
07                 (3)  harvest help and similar part-time or transient help;                                              
08                 (4)  a person employed as a sports official on a contractual basis and                                  
09       who officiates only at sports events in which the players are not compensated; in this                            
10       paragraph, "sports official" includes an umpire, referee, judge, scorekeeper,                                     
11       timekeeper, organizer, or other person who is a neutral participant in a sports event;                            
12                 (5)  a person employed as an entertainer on a contractual basis;                                        
13                 (6)  a commercial fisherman, as defined in AS 16.05.940;                                                
14                 (7)  an individual who drives a taxicab and whose compensation and                                      
15       written contractual arrangement is as described in AS 23.10.055(a)(13), unless the                                



01 hours worked by the individual or the areas in which the individual may work are                                        
02       restricted except to comply with local ordinances;                                                                
03 (8)  a participant in the Alaska temporary assistance program                                                           
04 (AS 47.27) who is engaged in work activities required under AS 47.27.035 other than                                     
05       subsidized or unsubsidized work or on-the-job training;                                                           
06 (9)  a person employed as a player or coach by a professional hockey                                                    
07 team if the person is covered under a health care insurance plan provided by the                                        
08 professional hockey team, the coverage is applicable to both work-related and                                           
09 nonwork-related injuries, and the coverage provides medical and related benefits as                                     
10 required under this chapter, except that coverage may not be limited to two years from                                  
11 the date of injury as described under AS 23.30.095(a); in this paragraph, "health care                                  
12       insurance" has the meaning given in AS 21.12.050;                                                                 
13 (10)  a person working as a qualified real estate licensee who performs                                                 
14 services under a written contract that provides that the person will not be treated as an                               
15 employee for federal income tax or workers' compensation purposes; in this                                              
16 paragraph, "qualified real estate licensee" means a person who is required to be                                        
17 licensed under AS 08.88.161 and whose payment for services is directly related to                                       
18       sales or other output rather than the number of hours worked;                                                     
19 (11)  a transportation network company driver who provides a                                                            
20 prearranged ride or is otherwise logged onto the digital network of a transportation                                    
21       network company as a driver; [AND]                                                                                
22 (12)  a person employed as an independent contractor; a person is an                                                    
23       independent contractor for the purposes of this section only if the person                                        
24                      (A)  has an express contract to perform the services;                                              
25 (B)  is free from direction and control over the means and                                                              
26 manner of providing services, subject only to the right of the individual for                                           
27 whom, or entity for which, the services are provided to specify the desired                                             
28 results, completion schedule, or range of work hours, or to monitor the work                                            
29 for compliance with contract plans and specifications, or federal, state, or                                            
30            municipal law;                                                                                               
31 (C)  incurs most of the expenses for tools, labor, and other                                                            



01            operational costs necessary to perform the services, except that materials and                               
02            equipment may be supplied;                                                                                   
03                      (D)  has an opportunity for profit and loss as a result of the                                     
04            services performed for the other individual or entity;                                                       
05                      (E)  is free to hire and fire employees to help perform the                                        
06            services for the contracted work;                                                                            
07                      (F)  has all business, trade, or professional licenses required by                                 
08            federal, state, or municipal authorities for a business or individual engaging in                            
09            the same type of services as the person;                                                                     
10                      (G)  follows federal Internal Revenue Service requirements by                                      
11 (i)  obtaining an employer identification number, if                                                                    
12                 required;                                                                                               
13 (ii)  filing business or self-employment tax returns for                                                                
14 the previous tax year to report profit or income earned for the same                                                    
15                 type of services provided under the contract; or                                                        
16 (iii)  intending to file business or self-employment tax                                                                
17 returns for the current tax year to report profit or income earned for the                                              
18 same type of services provided under the contract if the person's                                                       
19                 business was not operating in the previous tax year; and                                                
20                      (H)  meets at least two of the following criteria:                                                 
21 (i)  the person is responsible for the satisfactory                                                                     
22 completion of services that the person has contracted to perform and is                                                 
23 subject to liability for a failure to complete the contracted work, or                                                  
24 maintains liability insurance or other insurance policies necessary to                                                  
25 protect the employees, financial interests, and customers of the person's                                               
26                 business;                                                                                               
27 (ii)  the person maintains a business location or a                                                                     
28 business mailing address separate from the location of the individual                                                   
29                 for whom, or the entity for which, the services are performed;                                          
30 (iii)  the person provides contracted services for two or                                                               
31 more different customers within a 12-month period or engages in any                                                     



01                 kind of business advertising, solicitation, or other marketing efforts                                  
02                 reasonably calculated to obtain new contracts to provide similar                                        
03                 services; and                                                                                       
04                 (13)  a delivery network company courier who provides delivery                                      
05       services or is otherwise logged onto the digital network of a delivery network                                
06       company under AS 28.23.080.                                                                                   
07    * Sec. 2. AS 28.23.080(a) is amended to read:                                                                      
08            (a)  Except as provided in (b) of this section, a transportation network company                             
09       or delivery network company is not an employer of transportation network company                              
10 drivers or delivery network company couriers under AS 23.10.699, AS 23.20.520,                                      
11 or AS 23.30.395. A transportation network company driver or delivery network                                        
12 company courier is an independent contractor for all purposes and is not an employee                                
13 of the [TRANSPORTATION NETWORK] company if the [TRANSPORTATION                                                          
14       NETWORK] company                                                                                                  
15 (1)  does not unilaterally prescribe specific hours during which a driver                                               
16 or courier shall be logged onto the digital network of the [TRANSPORTATION                                          
17       NETWORK] company;                                                                                                 
18 (2)  does not impose restrictions on the ability of the driver to use the                                               
19 digital network of other transportation network companies or delivery network                                       
20       companies;                                                                                                    
21 (3)  does not restrict a driver from engaging in any other occupation or                                                
22       business; and                                                                                                     
23 (4)  enters into a written agreement with the driver or courier stating                                             
24 that the driver or courier is an independent contractor for the [TRANSPORTATION                                     
25       NETWORK] company.                                                                                                 
26    * Sec. 3. AS 28.23.180(1) is amended to read:                                                                      
27 (1)  "digital network" means any online-enabled application, software,                                                  
28       website, or system offered or used by a                                                                           
29 (A)  transportation network company that enables the                                                                
30            prearrangement of rides with transportation network company drivers; or                                  
31 (B)  delivery network company to facilitate offers for                                                              



01            delivery services;                                                                                       
02    * Sec. 4. AS 28.23.180 is amended by adding new paragraphs to read:                                                
03                 (7)  "company" means a transportation network company or a delivery                                     
04       network company;                                                                                                  
05                 (8)  "delivery network company" means a business entity that maintains                                  
06       a digital network used to facilitate offers for delivery services in the state;                                   
07 (9)  "delivery network company courier" or "courier" means a person                                                     
08 who is authorized to accept and fulfill offers through the digital network of a delivery                                
09 network company; "delivery network company courier" or "courier" does not include                                       
10 a person whose services require the use of a vehicle weighing more than 6,000                                           
11       pounds;                                                                                                           
12 (10)  "delivery services" means the pickup of an item from a location in                                                
13 the state and the delivery of that item to a location selected by the customer within 50                                
14 miles of the pickup location by walking or using a motor vehicle, a bicycle, a scooter,                                 
15 public transportation, or other means of transportation and the selection, collection, or                               
16 purchase of items by a delivery network company courier, as well as other services                                      
17 incident to delivery; "delivery services" does not include assistance with residential                                  
18       moving services;                                                                                                  
19 (11)  "offer" means the opportunity to perform delivery services for                                                    
20 compensation that a delivery network company presents to a courier through a digital                                    
21       network.
