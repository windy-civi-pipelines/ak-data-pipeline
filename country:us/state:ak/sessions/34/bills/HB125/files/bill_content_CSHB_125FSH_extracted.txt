AS 16.05.221(a) is amended to read: 04 (a) For purposes of the conservation and development of the fishery resources 05 of the state, there is created the Board of Fisheries composed of seven members 06 appointed by the governor, subject to confirmation by a majority of the members of 07 the legislature in joint session. The governor shall appoint each member on the basis 08 of interest in public affairs, good judgment, knowledge, and ability in the field of 09 action of the board, and with a view to providing diversity of interest and points of 10 view in the membership. The appointed members shall be residents of the state and 11 shall be appointed without regard to political affiliation or geographical location of 12 residence. The commissioner is not a member of the Board of Fisheries, but shall be 13 ex officio secretary. The governor shall appoint the members as follows: 14 (1) two members who represent commercial fishing and have 15 knowledge of and experience in the commercial fishing industry; 01 (2) two members who represent sport fishing and have knowledge 02 of and experience in sport fishing; 03 (3) two members who represent subsistence fishing and have 04 knowledge of and experience in subsistence fishing; 05 (4) one member of the scientific community who has knowledge of 06 and experience in fisheries management. 07 * Sec. 2. The uncodified law of the State of Alaska is amended by adding a new section to 08 read: 09 TRANSITION. A person who is a member of the Board of Fisheries on the day before 10 the effective date of this Act continues to serve until the expiration of the member's term. An 11 appointment for a vacancy that occurs on or after the effective date of this Act shall be in 12 accordance with AS 16.05.221(a), as amended by sec. 1 of this Act.
