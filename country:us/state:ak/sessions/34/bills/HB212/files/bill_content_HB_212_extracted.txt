AS 14.17 is amended by adding a new section to article 2 to read: 05 Sec. 14.17.530. Determination of local contribution made by a city or 06 borough school district. For purposes of calculating a local contribution made by a 07 city or borough school district, the local school board of the district shall define the 08 terms "student transportation," "nutrition services," "food services," "preschool, pre- 09 kindergarten, or other early education program," "career and technical education," 10 "before-school program," "after-school program," and "student activity." 11 * Sec. 2. AS 14.17.990(6) is amended to read: 12 (6) "local contribution" 13 (A) means appropriations and the value of in-kind services 14 made by a district; 01 (B) does not include appropriations or the value of in-kind 02 services made by a district for student transportation, nutrition services, 03 food services, a preschool, pre-kindergarten, or other early education 04 program, career and technical education, a before-school program, an 05 after-school program, or a student activity; 06 * Sec. 3. This Act takes effect immediately under AS 01.10.070(c).
